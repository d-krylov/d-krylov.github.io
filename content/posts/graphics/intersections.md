## Sphere

$$
| R_{O} + R_{D} t - S_{O} | = R \implies |R_{O} - S_{O}|^{2} + 2[(R_{O} - S_{O}) \cdot R_{D}] t + |R_{D}|^{2} t^{2} = R^{2}
$$

Тогда

$$
t^{2} - bt + c = 0, \quad b = 2(R_{O} - S_{O}) \cdot R_{D}, \quad c = |R_{O} - S_{O}|^{2} - R^{2}, 
$$

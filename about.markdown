# Experience

You can see my relevant experience in my CV. Here I describe old days.

My career started in 2009 in Institute for High Energy Physics (IHEP) in Protvino. It was specific experience. Most of my duties were related to physical labor. For example, I installed various equipment in the synchrotron. There was nothing useful for mind in this but when I walked around the synchrotron I felt like a Fallout character. When it become routine I decided that I had done something wrong.

I got a job at a company which developed spectrometric devices. This firm used STM32 microcontrollers in its products. I wrote the firmware for these microcontrollers. It was small company and as result we didn't use VCS, didn't have code review and made other terrible things. However, I got experience in embedded programming and got to know the architecture of microcontrollers quite well.

Next company was MCST. I developed tests on assembly language for CPU RTL model. It was CPUs with SPARC architecture. 

Then follows Samsung and you know where you can read about it.
